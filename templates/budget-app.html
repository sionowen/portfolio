Budget-App

The Assignment

Upon completion of the coursework of Prime we are given the task to create an independant project that we decide on. We have 2 weeks to document, design, scope, and develop the project.

My Project

For my project I decided to create an application to help people who are on a 0 balance checking account, living paycheck to paycheck, have more understanding of their finances.

The Problem

The specific problem that Budget-App is designed to address is where someone has a new unexpected expense come up and they currently have enough money in the bank to pay the new expense so they do. In actuality because they paid the new expense there is another expense later in time that they now cant afford. They were not aware that they were making a decision when they decided to pay the first bill.

The Solution

Budget-App has the user enter in their total liquid capitol as of the beginning of the month and then displays a calendar view, which I created using Moment.js and AngularJS. They may then enter incomes and expenses into the calendar on the days that the incomes and expenses occur. Budget-App displays the end of day total for each day and updates when users enter new transactions. When a user enters a new expense the calendar will show a negative balance at the end of any day where the user spends more than was available.

Technologies Used

Express.js, AngularJS, Node.js, Moment.js, Lodash, Passport, PostgreSQL, Bootstrap, and Heroku.
